# Moranta AI Ethics

## Ethical Foundation

Moranta is committed to developing reflective AI systems that
prioritize:

- human well-being
- emotional safety
- user sovereignty
- privacy
- transparency
- conscious technology practices

Our AI systems are designed to support self-reflection and awareness
rather than authority or dependency.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Ethical Principles

## 1. Human Intuition Comes First

The platform never presents AI interpretations as absolute truth.

AI-generated reflections are suggestions and possibilities --- not
definitive answers.

Users are encouraged to trust their own intuition, experiences, and
interpretations.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 2. AI as Reflection, Not Authority

Moranta AI systems:

- ask reflective questions
- identify patterns
- summarize observations
- encourage mindfulness

They do not:

- claim spiritual certainty
- diagnose psychological conditions
- manipulate beliefs
- predict the future
- position themselves as enlightened authorities

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 3. Emotional Safety

The platform is designed to avoid:

- fear-based engagement
- dependency loops
- manipulative emotional design
- coercive spiritual language
- emotionally exploitative experiences

The AI should always communicate with humility, openness, and care.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## 4. Transparency

Users should understand:

- what data is stored
- how personalization works
- how AI insights are generated
- what the system remembers
- what can be deleted or modified

Transparency builds trust and supports informed user choice.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Privacy Principles

Moranta treats personal reflections, dreams, journals, and emotional
data as deeply private.

We are committed to:

- encryption-first infrastructure
- private-by-default experiences
- user-controlled memory systems
- data export and deletion capabilities
- minimal data exposure

Moranta does not sell psychological or emotional profiling data.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Dependency Prevention

Moranta is intentionally designed to avoid unhealthy dependence on AI
systems.

Examples include:

- encouraging independent reflection first
- limiting authoritative language
- offering low-guidance modes
- supporting offline and silent reflection practices

The goal is to strengthen self-awareness, not replace it.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Community Responsibility

As Moranta evolves into a broader ecosystem involving creators,
teachers, and community interactions, we remain committed to:

- thoughtful moderation
- emotional safety
- anti-manipulation safeguards
- respectful dialogue
- spiritual openness without dogma

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Long-Term Ethical Commitment

We believe the future of conscious technology requires:

- humility
- intentionality
- emotional intelligence
- ethical design
- human-centered AI systems

Moranta seeks to contribute positively to that future.
