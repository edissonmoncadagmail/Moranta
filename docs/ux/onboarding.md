# Onboarding Experience

## Purpose

Introduce Moranta while building trust and establishing user control.

---

# First Impression

The first experience should communicate:

- safety
- curiosity
- calm
- empowerment

---

# Welcome Message

Moranta helps you explore:

- dreams
- mindfulness
- reflection
- personal growth

through human-centered AI.

The AI supports reflection.

You create meaning.

---

# Step 1: Intentions

Ask:

What brings you here today?

Examples:

- dream exploration
- mindfulness
- affirmations
- self-awareness
- emotional growth

---

# Step 2: AI Guidance Level

Allow users to choose:

## Light Guidance

Mostly questions.

---

## Balanced Guidance

Default.

---

## Deep Reflection

More observations and patterns.

---

# Step 3: Privacy Choices

Clearly explain:

- memory
- personalization
- data ownership

Users must opt-in.

---

# Step 4: Theme Selection

Default:

Moranta Classic

Optional:

- Ocean
- Forest
- Sunrise
- Night Sky

---

# Step 5: First Action

Guide users toward:

- creating a journal entry
- recording a dream
- creating an affirmation

Avoid overwhelming them with choices.

---

# Success Criteria

User should feel:

- informed
- respected
- safe
- curious
