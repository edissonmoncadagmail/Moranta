# Moranta Product Modules

## Purpose

Help users capture, explore, and reflect on dreams, emotions, recurring
symbols, and subconscious patterns.

## Core Features

- text dream journaling
- voice dream capture
- dream tagging
- emotional sentiment tracking
- recurring symbol detection
- lucid dreaming tracking
- sleep quality integration

## AI Role

- reflective prompts
- emotional summaries
- symbolic possibilities
- pattern recognition

## AI Boundaries

- no definitive interpretations
- no predictions
- no supernatural certainty
