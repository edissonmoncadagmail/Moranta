# Moranta MVP Definition

# MVP Objective

The goal of the Moranta MVP is to validate:

- reflective AI experiences
- spiritual growth workflows
- emotional engagement
- intentional daily usage
- human-centered AI interactions

The MVP should prioritize:

- simplicity
- emotional quality
- stability
- clarity
- meaningful user experiences

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# MVP Goals

The MVP should validate:

- user retention
- emotional resonance
- reflective AI usefulness
- affirmation engagement
- dream journaling behavior
- mindful routine formation

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Explicitly Excluded From MVP

To avoid excessive complexity, the MVP will NOT initially include:

- social networking
- open community systems
- creator marketplace
- livestream infrastructure
- advanced monetization
- complex gamification
- public profiles

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# MVP Success Criteria

Success indicators:

- repeat daily usage
- meaningful journaling engagement
- affirmation playback consistency
- positive emotional feedback
- user trust in AI interactions
- emotionally safe experiences

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# MVP Philosophy

The MVP should prioritize:

- depth over breadth
- emotional quality over feature quantity
- intentionality over engagement hacking
- trust over rapid growth
